# Project billing
In PPS, revenue for a project can be generated in two ways:

- Time and Material: invoices are generated based on costs incurred for labour, material and other expenses.
- Fixed Price: invoices are generated according to a predetermined sales value, which is not dependent on the amount of cost incurred.
