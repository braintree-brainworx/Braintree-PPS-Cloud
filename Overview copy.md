Braintree's core business systems are implemented on Microsoft Dynamics Business Central. The implementation includes 

- Standard modules from Business Central
- Enhancements to standard modules
- Braintree ISV modules
- Development of Braintree-specific modules
- Integrations and interfaces to external systems

# Projects and Resources
Professional services operations and billing are managed via the Projects and Resources module. Substantial enhancement has been done to these modules to better match the business processes.  Functions handled in this module are:

- Project management
- Service level agreements
- Support and ticket management
- Time sheet processing
- Expense claim management

# Financials and Back Office Management
Back office functions are handled through largely standard business central functionality, augmented by some Braintree ISV modules.

Standard modules implemented:
- General Ledger
- Banking
- Fixed Assets
- Sales and Receivables
- Purchases and Payables
- Inventory

Braintree ISV modules have been implemented:
- Vendor Reconciliation
- Debtors Collection Management
- Compliance Document Tracker
- Document Pack

# Integrations and Interfaces
The following integrations and interfaces have been implemented
- Integration to First National Bank to handle transactions to and from the bank.
- PowerApps integration to Exchange to handle communications on tickets
- PowerApps integration to handle 
- Manual interface to import billing for 3rd party licence subscriptions, and billing currently done on AutoTask. 

# Custom Braintree Modules
Custom development has been done to handle Braintree-specific processes:
- Substantial enhancement to Debit Order processing
- Capitalisation of resource costs into fixed assets (for development of own products)
- ISV Product Management
- Revenue accrual for Work in Progress
- Licence management of own products installed at customers.
- Expense claim management
